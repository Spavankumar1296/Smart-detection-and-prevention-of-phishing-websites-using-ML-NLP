Phishing Attack Types and Techniques

1. Spear Phishing
   Targeted attack aimed at specific individuals or organizations.

2. Clone Phishing
   A legitimate email is copied and malicious links are inserted.

3. Smishing
   Phishing conducted via SMS messages.

4. Vishing
   Phishing conducted through voice calls.

5. Credential Harvesting
   Fake login pages designed to capture user credentials.

6. Business Email Compromise (BEC)
   Attackers impersonate executives to request financial transfers.

Common Technical Indicators:
- High URL entropy
- Suspicious file extensions (.exe, .zip)
- Excessive use of hyphens
- High number of numeric characters
- Multiple form input fields
